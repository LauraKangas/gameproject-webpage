The planetary diet has the following goals:


- Ensuring adequate nutrition for the world's 10 billion people by 2050.
- Significantly reduce the number of deaths from malnutrition worldwide.
- To be environmentally sustainable.

The transition to a planetary diet is essential to achieving the Paris Climate Agreement and the UN Sustainable Development Goals (Agenda 2030).
